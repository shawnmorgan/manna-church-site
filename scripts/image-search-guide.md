## Recommended Image Sources

1. **For Church Buildings:**
   - Manna Church city websites (see locations.json for URLs)
   - Google Maps Street View
   - Yelp business photos
   - Facebook page photos

2. **For City Landscapes:**
   - Unsplash.com (free, high-quality)
   - Pexels.com (free, high-quality)
   - Pixabay.com (free)
   - Wikipedia Commons

## Image Specifications

- Format: JPG or PNG
- Minimum width: 800px
- Aspect ratio: 16:9 or 4:3
- Save to: `public/assets/images/locations/[location-slug].jpg`
